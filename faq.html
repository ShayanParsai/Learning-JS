Here's the Scoop on Our Crypto Tracker

We grab the latest trade prices from big crypto exchanges and spot the price differences for you. Check them out on our "Start Tracking" page. Here's what the colors mean:

  • White: If the price difference is tiny (0 to 0.5%), we mark it white. It's like a heads-up that it might not be worth the hustle.
  • Orange: See an orange? That's a 0.5 to 1% price gap. It's like a nudge saying, "Hey, there could be something here!"
  • Green: Green means go! Over 1% difference? That's your green light for a good arbitrage chance.

But hold up, don't just dive in! Make sure the exchanges have enough coins floating around for your trade.

How to Make the Magic Happen

  1. Buy Low: Snag that cryptocurrency for cheap on one exchange.
  2. Move It: Zip it over to where it's selling for more.
  3. Sell High: Cash in on the difference.
  4. Repeat: And just like that, you're ready for another round.

Tiny Details, Big Difference

Don't forget, moving and trading crypto comes with fees. Make sure the math adds up by checking the fees on both exchanges before you leap.

Usually, you'll want at least a 1% price difference to make it worthwhile, but it depends on the game you're playing (a.k.a. the currency and exchange).
